Entities List

Entities are special characters represented by a string starting with an ampersand (&) and ending with a semicolon (;).

  • < (Less Than Symbol)
  • © (Copyright Symbol)
  • & (Ampersand Symbol)
  • @ (At Symbol)

Note: Emojis can be directly copied and pasted into HTML, while entities are used for special characters that have specific meanings in HTML.
